App Name: Sensify Version: 0.0.1 Authors:

Moritz Migge, Niklas Philippi, Raoul Kanschat, Saphir El-Kaiy, Torben Kraft, Viktor Gorte

 Description:

Sensify is a light weight weather App bringing SenseBox data in your daily life:

  • - view data collected by the closest SenseBox

  • - view all Senseboxes in your Area

  • - chose your own SenseBox

  • - chose your preferences

  • - get notifications and stay cool
